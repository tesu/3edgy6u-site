About

Hi, I'm Jason, a sophomore studying Computer Science and Management at MIT. I lived in Fuzhou, Fujian, China for four years as a "satellite baby" before returning to Northern Virginia. I attended Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology, where I did algorithmic computing, participated in EconChallenge, and played trumpet. I've also studied Taekwondo since age 8 and have a third degree black belt.

Programming

My interest in programming stemmed from an early desire to create games. Upon entering high school, I taught myself some C++ to do competitive programming. I also took classes to learn Java and Android development. I worked with Ruby on Rails, my first MVC framework, during my internship in 2015. Later on, During my various side projects over the years, I have worked with Python, Django, PHP, C#, Node.js, and Meteor.

Web Development

I first became motivated to create my own website after seeing other users' websites on Sploder. My first website from 2009 serves as a painful reminder of how bad I was (am?) at web design. The website you're currently on is version 4.0 of 3edgy6u.com. The site was initially a self-hosted Wordpress blog, which was later replaced by a PHP site that embedded my Google+ feed. Version 3.0 was merely mobile-friendly reskin of the previous PHP site, while this version is a complete rewrite in Flask.
